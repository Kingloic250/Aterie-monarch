01 — Who We Are

Bringing your vision to life.

We are a Rwandan, Kigali-based production company committed to providing affordable production services for corporate, commercial, and entertainment projects. Our team of experienced professionals is dedicated to creating content of the highest quality that brings our clients' visions to life.

Atelier Monarch Ltd is a startup company aiming at becoming a reference Visual Storytelling and Brand-building Powerhouse for key industries such as Tourism, Hospitality, Culture, Sports, Real Estate, Corporate services and many others. We strongly believe in the empowerment of local talent, coupled with the expertise harvested in strategic cross-border partnerships.

Our Team is composed of professionals in Rwanda and Switzerland, working together with passion to exceed our partners' deepest expectations.

02 — How We Work

Your story, our expertise.

At Atelier Monarch, we truly value your business and strive to provide the best service possible. We take the time to understand your communication objectives and target audience, so we can produce something that reflects your brand values and the story you want to tell.

We are more than just a production company we are your media partner.

We understand that every client is unique, and that's why we assign a dedicated producer to each project. Our producers are here to guide you through every stage of your production, from the initial creative concept, to storyboarding, scripting, and final delivery.
