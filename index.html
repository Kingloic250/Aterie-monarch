01 — About

Stories that move people.

Ateriel Monarch is a Kigali-based creative film and production agency crafting visual narratives for brands, corporations, and NGOs across the African continent. From concept to final cut, we blend cinematic craft with cultural depth.

Our team of directors, cinematographers, editors, and motion designers brings every frame to life — whether it's a 15-second ad, a feature-length documentary, or a multi-camera live stream.
